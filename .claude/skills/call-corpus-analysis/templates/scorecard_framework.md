# Call Scorecard (generic, parametrized)

Adapt the `{{...}}` placeholders to your domain. Base: SPIN + BANT + Sandler, re-cut for the call
type you're grading (sales / recruiting / success / support).

## 2 head metrics (report these weekly)

1. **Speed-to-First-Call (S2FC)** — hours from `deal_created` to first call. Target ≤4h; critical >24h.
2. **Next-Step Booking Rate (NSBR)** — % of calls with a concrete booked next step. Target ≥70%.

## 6 blocks (0-10 each)

### Block 1 — Opening + Identity
| 0-3 | 4-6 | 7-10 |
|---|---|---|
| "Hello, is this X?" no company | names company, self, reason — but flat | + personal hook (referral, segment, project) |

Checklist: named the company in first 10s · named self · named the contact source ·
asked permission to talk · separate warm opener for inbound.

### Block 2 — Discovery (most important)
| 0-3 | 4-6 | 7-10 |
|---|---|---|
| 1-2 shallow questions | core qualifiers | + full situational depth before pitching |

List the **must-ask questions for your segment** here (e.g. for a trade hire: specialty, years of
experience, countries worked, project types, tools, reads blueprints, current employer & why
leaving, documents/status). **Rule: don't rush to pitch before discovery is done.**

### Block 3 — Trust + Anti-Fraud (under-rated)
| 0-3 | 4-6 | 7-10 |
|---|---|---|
| no pre-emptive trust message | generic "official / registered" | + concrete proof: registry id, public profile, a current employee to call, contract/insurance specifics |

Red flag: the rep says "we don't scam you" unprompted — that plants a negative the contact remembers.

### Block 4 — Offer Presentation
| 0-3 | 4-6 | 7-10 |
|---|---|---|
| just the price/rate | price + basics | + a **math calc** of the contact's concrete monthly/annual value + full terms breakdown + path forward |

Power move: right after the number, "here's your math specifically" — a calculator, not a generic figure.

### Block 5 — Objection Handling
Score = objections handled vs objections raised. Each unhandled = −2. Keep the top-5 rebuttals
(from the objection library) on a one-pager next to the rep.

### Block 6 — Close + Next Step
Commitment ladder, weak → strong:
1. "Call me if you change your mind" ❌
2. "I'll call you" 🟡
3. "I'll call Tuesday 3pm" 🟢
4. "Tuesday 3pm — so you've decided by then. Confirm?" ✅
5. "Monday 10am at our office — sending you the address by SMS now." 🔥

## Score → action
| Score | Status | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 50-60 | A+ | training library |
| 40-49 | A | tighten blocks 3 & 5 |
| 30-39 | B | lead calls back if hot |
| 20-29 | C | coaching on 2 missed blocks |
| <20 | F | "how not to" at the weekly review |

## Edge-case rules
- **Inbound:** discovery starts with a 30s "how can I help" + "what do you already know about us."
- **Returning contact (already in CRM):** first question must be "what changed since / what have you
  been doing" — else the pitch is instantly stale.
- **Lead >7 days old:** spend the first minute on the reason for the delay; a chunk of these already
  found another option — surface it proactively in the first 60s.
- **Wrong-fit specialist:** don't end the call — ask for a referral (seed a referral program).
