# Boosting Customer Service Productivity With HubSpot AI

- Being a support rep
is now easier than ever

with HubSpot's new AI tools.

You can say goodbye to routine tasks

and hello to more time for
complex and important issues.

Let's explore!

First on the list is Content Assistant

in the Knowledge Base.

You can edit articles

by highlighting any
text within your article

and clicking the Lightning
Bolt icon to find options

for rewriting, expanding, summarizing,

or changing the tone of your copy.

With AI-powered suggestions
right at your fingertips,

you can improve your Knowledge
Base articles in no time.

Next is the Conversations
Inbox where Support Assistant

is soon to become your
new favorite sidekick,

helping you chat like a
pro and save precious time,

generating the perfect response.

Imagine you're reviewing
your response to a chat

but feel like it could be better.

Simply highlight your text,
select the Lightning Bolt icon,

and you'll find several options
for optimizing your text.

And that's not all,

summarizing your support conversations

has never been easier.

No more playing catch up on
the full history of a ticket.

With one click, HubSpot
Support Assistant will review

the entire chat conversation
and write a brief summary.

You can then tag your teammate in the chat

to get them up to speed instantly.

This makes it easy to collaborate

and hand off tickets between team members.

How cool is that?

To get started, navigate
to the Comments tab

and click the Light Bulb icon.

Last but definitely not
least is the AI chatbot

that provides 24/7
support for your website.

Trained on your company's data,

these chatbots are ready
to assist your customers

and prospects anytime, day or night.

With AI handling the repetitive inquiries,

you can dedicate your valuable time

to tackling more complex issues

and providing top-notch support.

In your Chatflow app,
select Create Chatflow,

choose Website, then Live Chat + AI.

From there, assign your chatflow

to your AI chatbot, and that's it.

It's time to make your job easier

and more efficient with HubSpot AI.

Optimize your Knowledge Base articles

with Content Assistant,

generate and improve your
support messages in one click,

summarize your conversations effortlessly,

and let the AI chatbot
be your 24/7 support.

Remember, HubSpot AI is here
to make you a support hero,

allowing you to navigate

those tricky customer
conversations with ease.
