# Find New Prospects Using ChatSpot AI | HubSpot

- [Instructor] Let's explore how to find

new sales opportunities quickly
and easily using ChatSpot.

Let's say you need to find new prospects

that match your ideal customer profile.

Go to Templates and Prospecting,

and here you can discover
companies by industry,

location, technology, size,

years in business or what we call age

in the ChatSpot interface.

For this example, let's find companies

that are at least 10 years old

and have 250 or more employees.

Depending on the prompt,

ChatSpot will display a number of results.

If you'd like to research
any company in particular,

select the Research button,

and if that fits to
your prospecting needs,

click on Add to HubSpot to
move it right to your CRM.

No need to copy and paste,
it's one-click convenience.

Moving on, a good sales professional knows

they're only as good as their notes.

Luckily, ChatSpot makes this easy as well.

Go to Templates, select
Add Note to Contact.

You'll just need the
email of your prospect.

Then type or paste your
notes into the open field.

Hit Send, and you're done!

Onto the next thing.

Many companies use guidelines
in their sales process

to ensure contacts fit
their ideal customer profile

by knowing if they use
a specific technology

in their tech stack.

You can simply ask ChatSpot.

Go to templates, Company Insights,

and click on Technology Used.

Type in the domain

of the company you wish
to learn more about,

ChatSpot will return a report

of the technology
associated with that domain.

Use ChatSpot to grow your prospect list

by identifying the competitors
of your existing prospects.

Go to Templates, company Insights,
and click on Competitors.

Type in the domain of your prospect,

and then let ChatSpot work its magic.

You'll have a list of companies
similar to your prospect

that you could easily add
to your sales efforts.

There are many ways to use ChatSpot

for prospecting success,

dig in and see how it can work for you.
