# Revolutionize Your Marketing Campaigns With HubSpot AI

- Hey, marketers.

Are you ready to create
campaigns so seamlessly

and effortlessly that
your stress disappears?

We've got you covered

with HubSpot's new AI tools
built to help you every step

of the way from ideation
to campaign creation.

Let's explore.

First is the new website assistant.

This powerful tool lets
you create a one-page

website quickly and efficiently.

All you have to do is enter a few details

about your business and what
you envision for your website.

Then, in just a few seconds,

your page will be built in HubSpot.

Next is Campaign Assistant.

This free AI-powered tool is
a game changer when it comes

to generating copy

for your landing pages,
emails, social and ads.

To get started, visit
hubspot.com/campaign-assistant

and fill in the prompt

with your campaign's
topic, key selling points,

desired call to action and tone of voice.

Before you know it, you'll
have ready-to-use copy

for all of your campaign assets.

Content Assistant is
another new AI feature built

to help generate engaging email copy

without leaving HubSpot's
email marketing tool.

In your email editor,

type a forward slash, select
to generate a paragraph

or heading, describe your
topic, and let Content Assistant

create email copy that's ready to use.

You can also highlight existing
text to optimize your copy

by rewriting, expanding, summarizing

or changing the tone of your text.

And if you're struggling to come up

with a catchy subject line, don't fret.

With just one click,

Email Assistant can generate
a captivating subject line

for your email, but we're not done yet.

HubSpot's AI-powered social
assistant can also generate

engaging social posts
tailored to your networks.

In the social post editor,

click the lightning bolt
icon below your text box.

Add a few words about your post

and social assistant will do the rest.

No more spending too much time thinking

of wordy captions or searching
for the perfect hashtag.

This feature will help you
create social posts fast

and better on-brand.

So while you work

across marketing lanes
and juggle numerous tasks,

remember that HubSpot's AI tools are here

to make creating campaigns
feel effortless, efficient

and effective.

Give them a try

and start building your next
marketing campaign immediately.

(upbeat music)
