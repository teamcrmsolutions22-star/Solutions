# Store Sensitive Data in HubSpot Smart CRM

successful businesses thrive on data to drive growth and Delight customers however when it comes to sensitive data many organizations struggle to effectively and safely make use of it thankfully HubSpot provides a secure solution for storing sensitive customer data within the smart CRM this enables businesses like yours to utilize this data to fuel your growth manage compliance and mitigate potential disruptions here's how it works Begin by activating the sensitive data settings in your portal here you'll select the types of sensitive data you plan to store such as personal financial or medical data in case of the latter you'll be prompted to State whether you are a HIPPA covered entity or need a business associate agreement in order to comply with this United States data Protection Law once this is turned on you can start storing sensitive data in HubSpot smart CRM to do so simply create a property and designate it as sensitive what this does is add an extra layer of encryption for all data stored within it also ensures that only authorized users are able to view the data that it stores now that you have a secure place for your sensitive data you can use these sensitive properties more or less like any other property in HubSpot smart CRM with a few exceptions meaning you can now safely use sensitive data to power your automations campaigns and interactions and to light your customers with an exceptionally personalized experience if you want to know more about hubs SWAT sensitive data software like what types of sensitive data you can store in hubs SWAT and where you can or cannot use it please refer to our sensitive data terms or visit our trust Center
