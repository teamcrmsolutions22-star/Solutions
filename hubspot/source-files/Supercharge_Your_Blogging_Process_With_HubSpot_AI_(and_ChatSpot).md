# Supercharge Your Blogging Process With HubSpot AI (and ChatSpot)

- Hey, marketers, are
you ready to check out

how the new HubSpot AI
toolkit can take you

from a blank page to a
published and promoted blog post

in just minutes?

Let's get started.

First is a new feature

that will help you generate
blog content ideas.

In the top right select
Generate blog posts

and begin describing your topic.

This will then present a list

of possible titles and
keywords to choose from.

The best part, the tool combines
the power of generative AI

with SEMRush data to give you SEO insights

when choosing the best
idea for your next blog.

That way, you have a better
chance to rank higher

in search results with your blog content.

It's like having brainstorming sessions

with the world's smartest marketers.

From there, HubSpot will
provide an editable outline

that contains a title, description,

and subheadings based on your topic.

When you're ready,
HubSpot will then generate

the entire blog post

that you can refine to
your liking in the editor.

Now that you have a topic and outline,

let's review how
HubSpot's AI content tool,

content assistant, can help
you generate copy from scratch

or edit what you've already written.

Type a forward slash to generate new copy

or highlight existing copy to edit it

with the help of content assistant.

With slash and highlight commands,

you can generate or edit your
blog post text right in line.

No more jumping between tabs or apps,

now that's efficiency at its finest.

Blog posts often need images
to bring their words to life,

but it's not easy to always
find the right picture

or graphic for your post.

That's where Image Assistant,

HubSpot's AI powered image
generation tool, comes in.

Use the forward slash
and select Create images

or click the image icon
in the rich text toolbar.

Enter a description of your desired image,

choose a style from the dropdown,

and voila, you've got a
custom image for your post.

While you're in the creation stage,

be sure to leverage ChatSpot's
SEO keyword research feature

to find phrases or questions
that are related to your topics

and use those insights
to optimize your content.

Once the blog post is ready,

you'll wanna make sure
readers can find it easily.

Luckily, HubSpot has you covered.

With just a click of a button,

content assistant will
consider your written content

and automatically generate
an SEO-friendly title

and meta description.

Once your blog post is published

and optimized for search engines,

it's time to share it.

That's where the social
assistant feature comes in.

With just a few clicks,
you can create social posts

based on the content of your blog post.

No more racking your brains

for catchy captions or hashtags,

social assistant has you covered.

And there you have it, folks.

With our HubSpot AI toolkit,

you can go from thinking
about content ideas

to having a published and
promoted blog post in no time.

HubSpot's AI toolkit
