# Update CRM Records And Owners Using ChatSpot AI | HubSpot

- Balancing sales time with
data entry can be a challenge,

but not anymore.

Let's see how you can use ChatSpot

to easily update property values

and assign owners to records.

Go to Templates and over to the CRM tab.

Here you'll see a prompt
called Update contact.

Click into the prompt,

and you'll see a request for
key pieces of information.

Add the information needed and hit Send.

The contact email is the way
ChatSpot identifies the contact

and makes it possible to find the record

and easily update information.

If you'd like to assign yourself

as the owner to a record,

go back to the CRM Templates
and select Contact assignment.

Enter the email address
of the contact you want

and hit send.

Once the request is completed

you'll see the record has been
assigned successfully to you,

and you can even open a new window

to view this record in HubSpot.

Updating your CRM data
shouldn't take lots of time.

Use ChatSpot to speed up your workflow

and focus more time on sales.
