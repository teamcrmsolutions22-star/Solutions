# Writing A Blog Post Using ChatSpot | HubSpot AI

- Wish you could write
your blog posts faster?

ChatSpot lets you use AI

to create blogs in a fraction of the time.

Let's say you want to write
an article introducing

cybersecurity best practices to startups,

and you have a free guide with more info

that you want them to download.

To get started, click the templates tab,

then click marketing and select
generate blog from the list.

You'll see text boxes for
your company name, blog topic,

and what you want your
audience to do after reading.

The company name is
straightforward enough,

but the most important text
box here is the topic section

because this is how you'll
instruct the AI what to write.

As a quick tip, when you're
using AI to write content,

be sure to provide specific
and detailed instructions.

Rather than stop at a
short phrase for a topic,

take it a step further
and define what kind

of blog you want, in detail.

To make the blog truly
original and helpful,

you'll want to contribute your own ideas,

versus having the AI
come up with everything.

The third box is where
you'll define the call

to action or CTA.

Since we want readers

to download a cybersecurity
guide, let's add that here.

(upbeat music)

When you're ready, submit the prompt

and check out the results.

To finish this out, go ahead and copy

and paste into the website
or blog of your choice,

or you create a blog draft
right inside of HubSpot.

(upbeat music)

You may want to add personal
stories, adjust the tone,

or simply include relevant data points.

The output provided by ChatSpot provides

an excellent starting point
to speed up your process.

Your creativity and expertise are

what make it unique and valuable
beyond the factual data.

Consider adding statistics
and graphics as well.

Now, what if you want the blogs
to match your writing style

or brand voice without
needing to fix it each time?

If you click your profile in the top right

and click ChatSpot settings,

you can put a few blog
samples to the text box here

to help personalize ChatSpot's results.

Start writing your next
blog post at chatsspott.ai.
