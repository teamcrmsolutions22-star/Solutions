### Step-by-Step Onboarding Flow
1. Sign up for a Kommo account (free, no credit card required)
2. Begin the 14-day free trial — access all features at Enterprise level
3. Connect mailbox: Mail → Settings → Add email
4. Set up pipeline: Leads section → three-dot button → Edit pipeline
5. Create tasks: Calendar → New task (define type, responsible user, attach to lead/contact)
6. Connect messengers: Settings → Integrations → select desired channel
7. Import existing data: Leads → three-dot menu → Import (CSV or XLS)
8. Invite team members: Settings → Users → Invite

### Trial Period Details
- **Duration:** 14 days, free, no credit card required
- **Features available:** Full access to ALL Kommo features at Enterprise plan level
- **Only restriction:** Media storage limited to 10 GB during trial
- **AI agent trial:** Guided automatic setup available ONLY during the 14-day trial
- **AI sources during trial:** Up to 10 knowledge sources (paid plans: up to 100)
- **After trial:** Account reverts; must purchase paid plan to maintain access

### Free User Accounts
- Free users can be added to any paid account at no extra cost
- **What free users CAN do:** View and chat within lead/customer card profiles specifically shared with them
- **Limitations:** No access to full CRM functionality; no pipelines, reports, settings
- **How to grant access:** Admin sends internal chat message to free user to share a specific card
- **How to revoke access:** Click "Participants" in lower right of lead card → select free user → click red icon

### Partner Program
- **URL:** https://www.kommo.com/support/getting-started/partners-program/
- **How to join:** Fill application form → take self-paced course → pass certification test
- **Cost:** Free to join
- **Commission structure:**
  - 100% commission on first sale
  - 35% recurring commission on every subsequent subscription sold
  - Increases to 50% once sales to new customers reach $10,000
- **Benefit:** Partners receive a free Enterprise subscription for their own use
- **Target audience:** Entrepreneurs, marketing agencies, business consultants; no prior CRM experience required
- **Resources:** On-demand video courses, sales resources, partner portal
- **Services partners can offer:** CRM setup, automation design, chatbot development, client training — partners set their own prices and keep 100% of service revenue

## 3. CRM CORE FEATURES (DETAILED)

### Leads
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/leads/

Leads are the central objects in Kommo representing sales opportunities. Every lead has a profile containing contact information, company details, tasks, notes, emails, call recordings, chat conversations, and attached products.

**Default pipeline stages (auto-created):** Initial Contact → Offer Made → Negotiation → Closed-Won → Closed-Lost

**How to create leads:**
- Automatically from connected channels (messengers, webforms, etc.)
- Manually in the Leads section

**Plan limits:**
| Plan | Active Leads |
|------|-------------|
| Base | 2,500 |
| Advanced | 5,000 |
| Enterprise | 10,000 |

---

### Contacts & Companies
Contacts are profiles for individual people. Companies represent where contacts work. One company can have multiple contacts.

**Standard contact fields:** First name, Last name, Phone number (work, direct dial, mobile, fax, home, other), Email (multiple), Web address, Address

**Company fields:** Identification number, Phone number, Email, Web address, Address

**Plan limits:**
| Plan | Contacts |
|------|---------|
| Base | 12,500 |
| Advanced | 25,000 |
| Enterprise | 50,000 |

---

### Pipeline Setup
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/setting-up-a-pipeline/

**How to configure:**
1. Go to Leads section
2. Click the three-dot button (upper right corner)
3. Select "Edit pipeline"
4. Click an existing stage to rename, change color, or delete (click "x")
5. Drag stages to reorder
6. Create new stages as needed

**Important rules:**
- All pipelines end with two fixed stages: "Closed – Won" and "Closed – Lost" — **cannot be deleted**, only renamed
- Changes can only be made to Active and Closed stages
- Closed stages cannot be deleted or moved

**Multiple pipelines:**
1. Go to Leads → Automate
2. Click the pipeline name (upper left corner)
3. A list of existing pipelines appears — edit names, add new pipelines, or delete

**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/fields/

**Available field types:** Text, Number, Checkbox, List (single select), Multiselect list, Date, URL, Text area, Switch, Short address, Radio button, Select, Multiselect

5. Optionally make fields required for specific pipeline stages

**Plan limits:**
| Plan | Custom Fields |
|------|-------------|
| Base | 100 |

| Enterprise | 400 |

### Dynamic Fields
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/dynamic-fields/

Personalize messages using data pulled automatically from Lead cards. Insert dynamic field placeholders in message templates; they automatically populate with the lead's specific data when sent. Usable in: chat/email templates, broadcasts, forms, or regular messages.

---

### Tags
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/tags/

Classify leads/contacts using flexible categories (e.g., "priority leads", "first time buyers").

**Key uses:**
- Filter and bulk-edit leads by tag
- Export tagged groups
- Target specific automation sequences to leads with particular tags
- Set up Salesbot to trigger only for leads with specific tags

---

### Lists (Catalogs)
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/lists/

Create product/service catalogs that appear as a tab in every lead or customer card.

**How to create a custom list:**
1. Click on the Lists icon in the sidebar
2. At the bottom of the menu, click "Add list"
3. Name the list
4. Add elements (products/services)
5. Optionally categorize: three-dot menu → "Category" → name and save

---

### Dashboard
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/dashboard/

Analytical tool providing real-time data visualization. Fully customizable with widgets.

**Available widgets/reports:**
- **Win-Loss Analysis:** Won/lost percentages, average lead lifecycle, response times, where leads are lost most frequently
- **Consolidated Report:** New leads, leads moving to Closed-Won, conversion rate comparison
- **Activity Log:** Every change in the account — what was changed, who, and when
- NPS score averages and distributions
- Unanswered messages count
- Monthly target sales percentage
- Custom widgets (unlimited)

**How to configure:**
1. Click the "Setup" button in the dashboard
2. Add prebuilt widgets or delete unwanted ones
3. Drag widgets to change placement
4. Turn inbox filter results into Dashboard widgets for real-time display

---

### Tasks
Tasks must have a responsible user and a deadline (date + exact time). Can be linked to a lead, contact, or exist independently.

**How to create:** From any lead card, OR Calendar → New task → define type, responsible user, attach to entity

**Features:** Mark tasks complete, type the result, schedule follow-up task, sync with Google Calendar (mobile)

---

### Notes
Add structured or unstructured information to lead, contact, or company profiles. Can be pinned so they don't get lost.

**Note types:** common, call_in, call_out, service_message, geolocation, sms_in, sms_out, extended_service_message, Attachment, System

---

### Webhooks (Support Perspective)
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/webhooks/

**Setup Method 1 — Via Settings:**
1. Settings → Integrations
2. Click "Web hooks" (upper right)
3. Enter the WebHook URL
4. Select events to subscribe to

**Setup Method 2 — Via Digital Pipeline:**
1. In a pipeline stage: select "API: + Send webhook"
2. Select the trigger event
3. Enter the URL

**Rules:**
- Webhook must respond within **2 seconds** or it is considered undelivered
- Valid response: HTTP code 100–299
- **Plan availability: Advanced and Enterprise only**

---

### Additional CRM Features
- **Email integration:** Personal and shared mailboxes; Mail → Settings → Add email; group/ungroup email threads
- **Triggered emails:** Send emails automatically based on pipeline events
- **Import:** CSV/XLS import for leads, contacts, companies; field mapping templates; email notification on completion
- **Export:** CSV or XLS for leads, contacts, tasks; vCard for contacts/companies; **500 records per file** (larger exports split into multiple files)
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score chatbot; sends scale-of-1-to-10 prompt; requires at least one connected messenger; **Advanced and Enterprise only**
- **Appointment Scheduling:** Built-in scheduling with availability rules, minimum/maximum notice, scheduling interval; integrates with Google Calendar (two-way sync) and Calendly; AI agent can automate booking
- **VoIP/Telephony:** Click-to-call, call recording, call logging, smart forwarding; built-in browser call via WebRTC

### AI Agent — Full Overview & Setup

**Pages:**
- Overview: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/kommo-ai-agent/
- Automatic Setup: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/ai-agent-automatic-setup/
- Manual Setup: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/ai-agent-manual-setup/
- Launch & Use: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/ai-agent-usage/
- Packages: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/ai-agent-packages/
- After-hours: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/ai-agent-after-hours-support/
- Appointment Scheduling: https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/appointment-scheduling-ai-agent/

**What it is:** Built-in AI assistant that automatically handles customer conversations 24/7 using your business data. Average first response time: **3–8 seconds**.

**Where to access:** Settings → AI agent OR WhatsApp tab in left-hand panel

**4 Building Blocks:**

1. **Knowledge Sources**
   - Upload files (TXT, PDF, DOC formats), paste text, or import URLs
   - Enable, disable, unlink (disables without deleting), or delete sources
   - Trial: up to 10 sources | Paid plans: up to 100 sources
   - Agent can ONLY reply using configured sources

2. **Actions** — Guide agent behavior; each action detects specific customer messages and triggers a configured response

3. **Persona** — Defines how the AI agent communicates (role, tone, style)

4. **Integrations** — Connects Shopify or other stores for real-time product data, stock levels, and store policies

**Automatic Setup (Trial Only):**
- Available ONLY during the 14-day trial
- Auto-generates a ready-to-use agent from onboarding information (industry, company name, subdomain)
- No technical skills required

**Manual Setup Steps:**
1. Settings → AI agent
2. Choose "Create from scratch" or select a template
3. Configure Persona (role, name, tone, style)
4. Add Knowledge Sources (upload TXT/PDF/DOC, paste text, or import URL)
5. Set up Actions (what agent does when detecting specific messages)
6. Connect Integrations (e.g., Shopify for real-time product data)
7. Connect to messaging channels
8. Test the agent
9. Launch

**AI Agent Pricing:**
- Free during 14-day trial
- After trial: paid add-on on ANY plan
- AI agent packages provide a fixed amount of Credits for a Package Period
- Credits reset every **30 days** from purchase date (NOT on the 1st of each month)
- Unused credits **expire** — do NOT roll over
- Additional credits can be purchased at any time

### Salesbot Step & Action Types
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/salesbot-step-and-action-types/

| Action Type | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Message** | Send a text message to the client's chat |
| **Buttons (Show handler)** | Send message with button options for client to tap; specify message text and button labels |
| **Notes** | Add a note to the lead's card; select entity type; enter note text |

| **Conditions** | Filter step; set conditions that chat messages or contact fields must meet before proceeding |

### Salesbot Triggers
**Source:** https://www.kommo.com/support/crm/salesbot-triggers/

**4 Trigger Types:**

1. **Pipeline Triggers**
   - Launch when a new lead is created in a specific stage
   - Launch when a lead is moved to a specific stage
   - Launch after a specified time interval
   - Launch when the responsible user is changed

2. **Scheduled Triggers**
   - Launch at a specific date and time (useful for newsletters or promotions)

3. **Conversational Triggers**
   - Activates based on new messages in specified channels
   - Set a cooldown period to prevent overwhelming customers

4. **Keyword Triggers**
   - Trigger based on specific keywords in a client's message

**Tag conditions:** Restrict triggers to fire only for leads with specific tags

**Time configuration:** Set active hours during which the bot operates (e.g., only when staff is offline)

### Billing FAQ (Exact Q&A Pairs)

**Q: How does billing work?**
A: Your Kommo subscription applies to your entire account; all users must have the same subscription plan. Billing is per user per month.

**Q: What currency is billing in?**
A: Kommo bills in USD. You can also pay in Brazilian Reals (BRL), or find an official partner for other currencies.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit/debit cards (via Stripe and Ebury), PayPal (USD only, not available in Brazil).

**Q: How does auto-renewal work?**
A: Three days before subscription ends, an automatic renewal order is created. If payment is successful, subscription is renewed. Recurring payments auto-enabled after successful payment via PayPal, Stripe, or Ebury.

**Q: Can I renew manually?**
A: Manual renewal is unavailable if recurring payments are enabled. To renew manually, first disable recurring payments in the Billing section.

**Q: What is the refund policy?**
A: Kommo services are generally non-refundable (plan charges, WhatsApp Conversation Credits, add-ons, third-party integrations, one-time chatbot development). You CAN request a refund for unused periods by contacting support@kommo.com or via live chat — upon confirmation, account will be deactivated.

**Q: What about unauthorized charges?**
A: Contact Kommo support immediately; if verified as unauthorized/fraudulent, a full refund will be issued.

**Q: What is the minimum subscription period?**
A: 6 months. Discounts available for 9, 12, and 24-month periods.
