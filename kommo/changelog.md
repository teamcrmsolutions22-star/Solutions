# Kommo — Changelog / What's New (YouTube)

> Джерело: YouTube-канал Kommo, серія "What's New in Kommo" (вересень 2024 — травень 2026)
> Файли: `kommo/source-files/What*`

---

## September Edition (2024)

- **Ko Copilot — unanswered conversations check**: Ko Copilot can now check unanswered conversations with default commands, making sure no lead gets left behind.
- **AI Agent setup — readymade templates**: Setting up your AI agent is faster than ever — choose from readymade templates, define rules for stable performance, decide exactly where it will be active, and set your agent's tone of voice.
- **TikTok messaging ads — lead tracking & profile enrichment**: Track leads from TikTok messaging ads and automatically enrich their profiles with tracking data, helping you understand your audience, optimize ad spend, and drive higher ROI.
- **Click-to-WhatsApp ads — tracking data autofill**: Kommo autofills lead profiles with tracking data from click-to-WhatsApp ads, helping you measure and optimize campaigns with ease.
- **WhatsApp pricing calculator — quantity field & updated pricing terms**: A new quantity field was added and pricing terms updated, making it simple to calculate the cost of broadcasts.
- **Navigation improvement — Templates and Bot sections separated**: The Templates and Bot sections are now separate, as are WhatsApp templates and Ko templates.

## October Edition (2024)

- **TikTok Ads Manager integration**: Every lead from TikTok Instant Forms flows straight into your Kommo pipeline — automatically tagged, organized, and ready for follow-up.
- **Broadcasting — Segments feature**: New segments feature makes it easy to create, edit, and manage audiences right inside the broadcast builder; apply filters, preview message counts, and instantly see the cost of a WhatsApp broadcast before sending.
- **Marketplace — Yay Forms**: Capture leads with conversational forms.
- **Marketplace — Forminator Forms (WordPress by CRM App)**: Seamlessly connect WordPress forms.
- **Marketplace — Appmax by 7Club**: Automate sales messages and payments in real time.
- **Marketplace — PowerBI Connector by Tigratika**: Sync Kommo data directly to PowerBI.
- **Marketplace — AI Solutions tab**: New AI Solutions tab in the marketplace to find all AI-powered integrations in one place.

## November Edition (2024)

- **WhatsApp template creation window — redesign**: Fresh and simpler look; new step descriptions for each template type, demo animations, helpful hints. Variables in the text area, emoji block, and text styling options included.
- **Ko AI Agent — expanded CRM actions**: The Ko AI agent can now launch sales bots, move leads between pipeline stages, assign responsible users, create or delete tags, create tasks, and edit fields — in addition to chatting with clients.
- **TikTok — comments and reactions**: New leads can come directly from comments on TikTok posts; react or reply right from Kommo.
- **Marketplace — 14 new integrations**, highlights:
  - **Leelu.ai via Donor Tools**: Automate routine tasks and speed up lead processing.
  - **Late Node**: No-code automation platform for effortless workflows.
  - **User Time Zones by MYI**: For teams working across different time zones.

## December Edition (2024)

- **Redesigned left menu**: Cleaner, faster navigation. Key improvements:
  - **Clear sections**: Tools grouped into logical hubs — Communication, Pipelines, AI and Automation, and Insights.
  - **Consistent navigation**: Hovering and clicking behave the same across all areas.
  - **Collapsible design**: Expand for full detail or keep it slim to maximize workspace.
  - **Smarter settings**: Profile, workspace, and billing centralized for easier account management.
- **WhatsApp broadcast — Test Mode**: Safe way to test broadcast messages before going live. Scan QR code, send a quick message, test source auto-connects; launch broadcast to yourself to see full sending flow.
- **WhatsApp — Sender's name display**: Choose whether the sender's name appears in the chat. Customer sees the sender's name from the user profile.
- **AI Agent — Follow-up messaging**: AI agent can send follow-up messages even without an incoming prompt. Set a condition and the agent will send a message or perform a CRM action — updating fields, changing stages, or launching bots.
- **TikTok integration — Chat prompt configuration**: Configure TikTok chat prompts directly in the integration widget.

## February Edition (2025/2026)

- **AI Agent — task creation from conversations**: AI agent creates and sets tasks directly from conversations based on what the client says. Automatically schedules work, dynamically sets task type, date, time, and description.
- **AI Agent — automatic customer data collection**: AI agent automatically collects and fills customer data during a conversation — detects key information and dynamically updates specified fields in real time.
- **Templates section — search and filters**: Search and filter options added to the templates list.
- **Broadcast statistics**: Full visibility and real-time control for campaigns:

- **AI Agent — dedicated pinned button in left side menu**: AI Agent now has its own pinned button in the left sidebar.

## April Edition (2025/2026)

- **AI Agent — voice message support**: AI agent can work with voice messages. Convert voice into text; AI understands incoming voice messages and responds instantly in text.
- **Google Calendar integration — full two-way sync**: Full two-way sync between Google Calendar and Kommo. Share a booking link; once a client selects a time, a new lead is automatically created in Kommo with all their details.
- **Billing — updated pricing plans**: Updated pricing plans including a new plan for large-sized businesses and new features in existing plans.
- **TikTok — disable leads from comments**: Disable leads from comments within the integration widget.
- **Marketplace — 10 new integrations**, highlights:
